* For data submissions and/or corrections, please be specific and attach documentation from a published source. Acceptable formats include .docx, .md, .pdf and .txt. To contribute information that has not been published in a scholarly source, please explain how you have come to know the details. Information uncorroborated by a scholarly source will be included but will be identified as such in the sites' records.
